THE SPRING EXCEPTION
Every transition in the noir system snaps at 0.12–0.20s. The grade letter is the ONE exception. It appears with cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) for 400ms. The slight overshoot gives the grade physical weight. An "F" that bounces slightly feels heavier than one that fades in. This is the payoff for 4 minutes of investment.
